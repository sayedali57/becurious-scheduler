Auto-schedule the week
This rebuilds the schedule — read carefully before continuing.

This will automatically place 0 unscheduled student session(s) into car slots, minimizing drive time and grouping students by teacher where possible.

⚠ Warning: If you tick "Clear the existing schedule first", the entire current week is wiped before placement. This cannot be undone. Only use this if you really want to start from scratch.
How it works:
1. Only pool students (those not yet fully scheduled) are placed — existing schedule is kept unless you tick the box below.
2. Each student is assigned to the closest car on a day the teacher is available, respecting Max/day and same-day rules from ⚙ Settings.
3. 1-on-1 students get their own slot. Group students are batched with other group-type students who share the same teacher (up to the group-size limit in Settings).
4. Students of the same teacher are chained consecutively to reduce handoff drives.
5. Auto pickup slots are added after each drop (if enabled in Settings).
When a specific branch is picked, only that branch's pool students get placed — other branches' existing slots stay untouched. Supervisors can run this per branch and then hand-tweak the result.
You can change or remove this PIN in ⚙ Settings → Auto-schedule safety.
