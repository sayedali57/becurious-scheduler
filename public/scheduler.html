💡 Suggested upgrades for next round
High impact:
1. Firebase real-time sync — replace localStorage with a cloud database so all coordinators see the same schedule simultaneously. ~1 developer day.
2. WhatsApp driver dispatch — auto-generate a WhatsApp message for each driver summarising their day: stops, times, student names. One tap sends it.
3. Weekly visit tracker — dashboard showing each child's total visits this month vs. contracted visits. Flag children who haven't been visited.
4. Teacher workload view — bar chart of sessions per teacher per week. Immediately shows who is overloaded or underutilised.

Medium impact:
5. Parent confirmation toggle — mark each session as "parent confirmed / not home / rescheduled" during the day from a phone-friendly view.
6. Recurring schedule copy — copy a full week's schedule to next week with one click, then adjust exceptions.
7. Kuwait traffic time-of-day model — adjust travel times based on whether the slot is before or after 10am rush and before or after 3pm school pickup.

Lower priority but useful:
8. Colour-coded branch heatmap on the student map showing coverage gaps.
9. Printable daily driver sheet (A4 PDF per car).
10. Substitution mode — if a teacher is absent, quickly reassign her students to another teacher for the day without touching the main schedule.
